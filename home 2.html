The Rise of the Coffee house

Whatever the actual origin of coffee, its stimulating effect undoubtedly made it popular in Arabia. The Coffee Belt

The "Coffee Belt" refers to the equatorial regions of the world where coffee is most successfully grown, typically between the Tropics of Cancer and Capricorn. This area provides the perfect combination of altitude, rainfall, and temperature for coffee plants to thrive.
